Chicken Gyros Recipe

Recipes

Ingredients:

  • 2lbs chicken thigh fillets
  • 4 pcs pita bread
  • olives
  • pickled onions

    • Marinade:

  • 3 large garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp white wine vinegar
  • 3 tbsp lemon juice
  • 3 tbsp Greek yogurt
  • 1 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp salt
  • Black pepper (to taste)

    • Tzatziki:

  • 2 cucumbers
  • 1 1/4 cups Greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • Black pepper (to taste)

Instructions:

  1. Place the marinade ingredients into a bag with the chicken. Massage to mix. Marinate for 3 hours.
  2. Cut cucumber in half length wise. Scoop out seeds. Grate cucumber into a bowl.
  3. Add remaining ingredients for tzatziki and mix to combine.
  4. Brush outdoor grill with oil then preheat over medium high heat.
  5. Cook chicken for 2 to 3 minutes each side, until internal temperature is around 155.
  6. Remove chicken from grill and put on plate. Let sit to come up to temperature.
  7. Cut chicken into strips.
  8. Get pita bread out and put chicken on it, then olives, pickled onions, and tzatziki. Enjoy!
